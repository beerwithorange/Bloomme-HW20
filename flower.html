Return Policy

We will gladly accept refunds up to 7 days for store merchandise. All items being returned must be in the same condition as when purchased. A receipt is required. Please note that ALL plants, Artist pots, Terrariums and potted plants are non-refundable. Please Note: We are happy to hold your items after purchase for up to 5 days. (including online purchases) After 5 days, your items will be forfeited & you will not receive a refund. Plants waiting to be repotted should be picked up within 5 days after we call for pick up. We are not responsible for the care of your plants after purchase. With an exception of plants waiting to be repotted.
